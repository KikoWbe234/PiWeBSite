Tutorial

If you are interested in trading in your Pi for any service, here is a quick tutorial explaining how to do it and understand it!

First, you need to open your Pi browser.If all of your mainnet checklists are completed you can directly go to your wallet.

Once you are there you need to open your pi wallet with your pi pass phrase or fingerprint ID.Do not share your pi passphrase with anyone we do not ask you for your passphrase

When you open your pi wallet you will see a balance number of your total migrated pi, however this does not mean that you can use this pi.Right below the balance number you can see Available Balance:0.28 pi that is the actual pi amount you can transfer to other people.

If you see your balance is x amount and your available balance is 0 that means you have your pi locked up.

Pi automatically locks up a portion of pi every time on preset amounts.You must turn automatic lockup off to prevent your pi from further lock ups.

You can check your locked up pi to see when it is available on the wallet.
